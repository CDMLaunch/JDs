Data Science Analyst for the UPS
The HR Data Science Analyst implements advanced analytics models and solutions to yield predictive and prescriptive insights from data. He/She works with a team responsible to research and implement advanced analytic models. This position leverages industry standard machine learning and data visualization tools to transform data and analytics requirements into predictive solutions. The HR Data Science Analyst builds and runs models designed to solve business problems.
